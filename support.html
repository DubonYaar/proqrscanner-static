Need Help?

We're here to help you get the most out of Pro QR Scanner.

Contact Support

If you're experiencing a problem, have a question about subscriptions, or want to suggest a new feature, please reach out to us.

support@proqrscanner.online

When contacting us, it helps if you include:

  • Device model – for example: iPhone 15
  • iOS version – you're currently running
  • App version – of Pro QR Scanner
  • Description – a short explanation of the issue
  • Screenshots – if possible, to help us understand the problem

Common Topics

  • Subscriptions & Pro features – How to unlock unlimited scans and templates.
  • Scanning issues – QR code not recognized or too small.
  • Creating & designing QR codes – Colors, logo, shapes, and export options.
  • Sharing QR codes – How to save or share QR codes with other apps.

If your question isn't covered here, just send us an email and we'll do our best to respond as soon as we can.
